The Beginning

A founder's obsession with honest beauty

Munera was born in 2018 out of frustration. Our founder, Dr. Layla Munera, a cosmetic chemist with 15 years of experience, was tired of products full of empty promises and hidden ingredients.

She spent two years in her lab, formulating products that were truly effective, truly transparent, and truly kind to the planet. The name Munera — meaning "gifts" in Latin — reflects our belief that great skin is a gift anyone deserves.

Today, Munera is trusted by over 15,000 customers across 30 countries, and every single product is still made to the same exacting standards Dr. Layla set in that first lab.

What We Stand For

Our Commitments

Clean Formulas

Every ingredient is chosen for a reason. We publish our full ingredient lists and never use harmful fillers, synthetic fragrances, or harsh preservatives.

Cruelty Free

We are certified by the Leaping Bunny Program. Not a single Munera product has ever been tested on animals, and we never will.

Sustainability

Our packaging is made from 80% recycled materials. We offset 100% of our shipping carbon and donate 1% of revenue to ocean cleanup initiatives.

Inclusivity

Great skin isn't a privilege. Every formula is designed and tested across all skin types, tones, and concerns so that everyone can glow.
