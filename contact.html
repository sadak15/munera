Common Questions

How long Last names shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express (1–2 days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 days.

Can I return a product?

Yes — we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

All Munera products are dermatologist-tested and fragrance-free. Products marked "sensitive" on our shop are specifically formulated for reactive skin.

Do you offer samples?

Yes! We include a complimentary sample with every order. You can also request specific samples when you email us.
